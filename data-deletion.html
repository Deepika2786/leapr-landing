Data Deletion

How to delete your data from Leapr — fully and permanently.

What data does Leapr store?

When you use Leapr, we store the following information:

  • Your name, email address, and profile information
  • Your resume content (used only for gap analysis)
  • Your career analyses and gap reports
  • Your cohort memberships and community posts
  • Your job applications and saved jobs
  • Your connections and coffee chat history
  • Your notification preferences and settings

Delete your account from the app

The fastest way to delete all your data is directly from within the Leapr app. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Steps to delete your account:

1
Open app.leapr.co and sign in to your account
2
Go to your Profile page
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account"
4
Confirm the deletion — your account and all data will be permanently removed

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to your account or prefer to request deletion via email, contact us directly. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm when your data has been deleted.

Send your deletion request to our privacy team

Email privacy@leapr.co →

What happens after deletion?

  • Your profile and personal information is permanently deleted
  • Your analyses, roadmaps, and gap reports are removed
  • Your posts and cohort activity are anonymised or deleted
  • Your email is removed from all marketing lists
  • Backups are purged within 30 days
  • We retain only what is legally required (e.g. billing records)

Questions?

If you have any questions about your data or this process, email us at privacy@leapr.co and we'll respond within 48 hours.
